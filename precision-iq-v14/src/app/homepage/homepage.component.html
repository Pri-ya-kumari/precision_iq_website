Our Technology

Precision Diagnostics, Powered by Metabolomics

At PrecizionIQ, we combine advanced metabolomics with gentle, non-invasive testing to provide early prenatal diagnostics. Our platform identifies subtle changes in maternal metabolism to detect conditions like Down, Edwards, Patau, Turner, and Klinefelter syndromes, giving parents peace of mind while keeping tests safe and accessible.

Our Impact

Offering A Healthier Start for Every Child by Transforming Prenatal Care Across the Globe

Redefining What’s Possible in Maternal Health

Each year, millions of expecting families in India and around the world are left in the dark about the health of their unborn children. Current prenatal diagnostic options are often invasive, expensive, or simply unavailable—particularly in low-resource settings.

At PrecizionIQ, we’re breaking these barriers by offering a test that is accurate, non-invasive, affordable, and usable in the first trimester, empowering families with clarity, time, and choice.

A Healthier Start for Every Child

By making advanced prenatal testing more accessible, we aim to:

  • Reduce anxiety for expecting parents
  • Provide clinicians with clear, early insights
  • Enable better-informed pregnancy decisions

Beyond Chromosomal Disorders

Our long-term vision goes beyond current prenatal conditions. As we expand our platform, we aim to enable early screening for other serious inherited and developmental conditions, such as:

  • Cystic fibrosis
  • Muscular dystrophy
  • Neurodevelopmental disorders (including autism)
