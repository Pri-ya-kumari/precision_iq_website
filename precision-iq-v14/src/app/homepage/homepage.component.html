A New Era in Prenatal Care

At PrecizionIQ, we are reimagining the future of prenatal diagnostics. Our mission is to empower every expecting family with access to fast, accurate, and affordable diagnostic tools—delivered with compassion and backed by science.

Harnessing cutting-edge high-resolution mass spectrometry and deep expertise in human metabolomics, our technology enables non-invasive detection of chromosomal abnormalities as early as six weeks into pregnancy.

Let’s make prenatal health a universal right—not a privilege.

Driven by Purpose. Backed by Science

PrecizionIQ was founded by a team of scientists and entrepreneurs with deep roots in metabolomics, biomarker discovery, and clinical diagnostics. With academic and professional pedigrees from globally respected institutions like IIT, Cornell University, and Baylor College of Medicine, our leadership brings together decades of experience across molecular biology, analytical chemistry, data science, and biotech commercialization.

Our Technology

Precision Diagnostics, Powered by Metabolomics

At PrecizionIQ, we are harnessing the power of high-resolution mass spectrometry (HRMS) and metabolite-based biomarkers to transform how chromosomal abnormalities are detected during pregnancy. Our platform targets a range of conditions—including Down syndrome (Trisomy 21), Edwards syndrome (Trisomy 18), Patau syndrome (Trisomy 13), Turner syndrome, and Klinefelter syndrome—which collectively impact 1 in every 5 pregnancies globally. By identifying subtle changes in the maternal metabolic profile, we enable diagnostics that are not only early and non-invasive but also highly accurate and accessible.

Our Impact

Redefining What’s Possible in Maternal Health

Each year, millions of expecting families in India and around the world are left in the dark about the health of their unborn children. Current prenatal diagnostic options are often invasive, expensive, or simply unavailable—particularly in low-resource settings

At PrecizionIQ, we’re breaking these barriers By offering a test that is accurate, non-invasive, affordable, and usable in the first trimester, we empower families with clarity, time, and choice—exactly when it matters most.

A Healthier Start for Every Child

Our work strengthens healthcare systems and supports families by reducing:

  • The burden of late-stage clinical interventions
  • The emotional and financial toll on expecting parents
  • The barriers to early prenatal care in underserved regions

Together, we're building a future where maternal and fetal health is safeguarded from the very start—with compassion, precision, and purpose.

Beyond Chromosomal Disorders

Our long-term vision goes beyond current prenatal conditions. As we expand our platform, we aim to enable early screening for other serious inherited and developmental conditions, such as:

  • Cystic fibrosis
  • Muscular dystrophy
  • Neurodevelopmental disorders (including autism)
