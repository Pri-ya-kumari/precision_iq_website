Our Technology

Precision Diagnostics, Powered by Metabolomics

PrecizionIQ uses high-resolution mass spectrometry and metabolite biomarkers to transform prenatal screening for chromosomal abnormalities. Our non-invasive platform detects conditions like Down, Edwards, Patau, Turner, and Klinefelter syndromes, through subtle changes in maternal metabolism.

Ultra-Early Detection

Detects biomarkers in maternal blood and urine as early as six weeks into gestation, well ahead of conventional methods

High Accuracy

By mapping disease-specific metabolic fingerprints, our technology delivers >95% accuracy in distinguishing affected from unaffected pregnancies

Non-Invasive Testing

A simple blood or urine test—no needles, no procedures, no risk.

Affordability at Scale

our tests are priced to serve both urban and rural communities, especially in emerging healthcare systems

AI-Enhanced Biomarker Discovery

Each sample is screened across hundreds of thousands of molecular features using machine-assisted data filtering, enabling the identification of a focused panel of highly specific, clinically validated markers.
